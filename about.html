About Get Kinder

About.

The inspiration behind Get Kinder started with a simple question: What if kindness was contagious? Today, our world feels like mutual respect and positivity are at an all-time low, and we want to change that. To shift that direction, we need a world where kindness is the norm.

Now, Get Kinder makes it easy for individuals to make kindness a habit and share, celebrate, and inspire kindness in others. We use technology as a force for good to scale impact to create ripple effects that rebuild social connections and reduce loneliness.

Mission.

Get Kinder is here to make the world kinder and happier. We do this by making it easy to be kind, as this doesn’t just brighten someone else’s day — it transforms yours! We keep this going by using technology for good by celebrating and rewarding your positive impact all the way - doing good should feel good.

Vision.

At Get Kinder, we believe in the power of kindness to transform lives. Our platform is dedicated to fostering meaningful connections, uplifting communities, and making the world a more compassionate place— rewarding one act of kindness at a time.
